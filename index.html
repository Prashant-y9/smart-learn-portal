Smart Learn Portal

Smart Learn Portal is a centralized digital platform where the Class Representative can easily upload notes, assignments, timetables, and announcements, and students can access them anytime. It improves classroom communication by keeping all important updates organized in one place. The portal also includes an AI assistant, SmartAI, which helps students with summaries, explanations, and study support. Overall, it ensures that the entire class stays connected, informed, and well-managed.
